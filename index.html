Course Curriculum

Every section, covered.

The Common App has a lot of moving parts. This course walks through each one with real examples and clear direction — so nothing gets left to guesswork.

Module 01

Introduction & Strategy

Overview of the Common App, how colleges use it, and how to approach the whole process strategically from the start.

  • What colleges are really looking for
  • Timeline & planning your application
  • Common mistakes to avoid early
Module 02

Profile & Background Sections

The basics of the Common App profile, demographics, and how to fill in your personal information accurately and strategically.

  • Residency and citizenship sections
  • Family background questions
  • How to answer sensitive questions
Module 03

Academics & Testing

How to present your GPA, transcripts, and test scores in the best possible light — even if they aren't perfect.

  • Entering grades and course rigor
  • SAT / ACT reporting strategy
  • Handling course explanations
Module 04

Activities & Extracurriculars

The activities section is one of the most underutilized parts of the Common App. Learn how to tell your story through what you've done.

  • Ranking your activities strategically
  • Writing 150-character descriptions that pop
  • Showing leadership and impact
Module 05

The Personal Essay

The heart of the application. Learn how to choose a topic, find your voice, and write an essay that admissions officers remember.

  • Breaking down the 7 prompts
  • Brainstorming your unique story
  • Draft, revise, and finalize
Module 06

Additional Information Section

Most students leave this blank. Learn when and how to use it strategically to address gaps, context, or extra achievements.

  • When to use vs. leave blank
  • Addressing low grades or gaps
  • Adding accomplishments that didn't fit
Module 07

Recommenders & School Reports

How to choose the right recommenders, approach them professionally, and set them up to write the strongest letters possible.

  • Choosing your recommenders wisely
  • How to ask (with templates)
  • What to share with your recommenders
Module 08

Supplements & Final Review

Supplemental essays for each school, "Why Us" essays, and a complete checklist for reviewing your application before submitting.

  • Writing school-specific essays
  • "Why us" essay formula
  • Final submission checklist
